Tattoo artists in London: FAQs

How much does a tattoo cost in London?
London prices vary by artist, style and size. Many studios charge an hourly rate, often around £80 to £150 per hour, with a typical minimum charge for small pieces. Large custom work is usually priced per session or as a full project. Uploading your design and getting quotes is the best way to see real prices for your idea.
Are tattoo artists in London licensed?
In the UK, tattoo studios and artists must be registered with their local council and follow strict hygiene and safety standards. We check registration at sign-up, but you can always ask an artist to confirm their council registration and insurance before you book.
How do I book a tattoo artist in London?
Upload your design or describe your idea, tell us the placement and rough size, and available London artists will respond with quotes and availability. Review their portfolios and reviews, then choose who to book with. It is free to post.
How far in advance should I book?
Popular London artists can have waiting lists of several weeks or months, especially for large custom pieces. Smaller or flash designs are often available sooner. Posting early gives you the best choice of artists and dates.
Do London studios require a deposit?
Most studios take a deposit to secure your appointment, which is usually deducted from the final price. The amount and refund terms vary by studio, so check before you confirm your booking.
